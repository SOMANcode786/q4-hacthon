# Introduction to Digital Twins

This module covers the concept of digital twins in robotics, creating virtual replicas of physical robots and environments for simulation, testing, and development.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand digital twin concepts in robotics
- Learn the benefits of simulation-first development
- Explore digital twin applications in humanoid robotics
- Understand the connection between physical and virtual systems
